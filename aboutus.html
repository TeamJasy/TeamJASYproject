Yajahira "Yari" Velazquez


I am an army veteran of 17 yrs, a role model to 2 teenagers, and a forever student. I say forever, because everything evolves and if we don't take the time to evolve with these changes, we'll get left behind. Prior to changing my career path, I was in medical school. When medical school was no longer an option, I turned my attention to teaching myself web and graphic design. That lead to my business as a one-stop shop digital agency. It was designing websites on these plug and play platforms, that sparked my interest in software development. I want to be able to truly give my clients a personalized design that reflects their vision. I believe software development is going to open opportunities that I hadn't even considered possible. I chose software development specifically because I enjoy creating things and all things tech, but also because I have already missed so much of my children's lives while in med school, that this opened the door to remote employment. Ultimately, I would like to use my extensive medical knowledge and combine it with software development and create the next web application used as a standard in the healthcare industry.
